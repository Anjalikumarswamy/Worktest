Difference between Aggregation and Compostion 
Aggregation implies to a relationship where the child can exist idependently of the parent. It is "Has- a" relation. This is weak association.
Compostion  implies to a relationship where the child cannot exist idependently of the parent. It is "Part-of" relation. This is strong association.
